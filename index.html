Team Fortress 2

Team Fortress 2 (TF2) is a popular team-based first-person shooter game developed by Valve Corporation. Known for its distinctive art style, humorous approach, and diverse cast of characters, TF2 offers fast-paced multiplayer gameplay where players choose from nine unique classes, each with its own strengths and abilities. The game features objective-based modes, emphasizing teamwork and strategic gameplay. With a vibrant community, regular updates, and a free-to-play model, Team Fortress 2 continues to captivate players worldwide since its initial release in 2007.
